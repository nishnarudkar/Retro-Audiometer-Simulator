# Product Overview

## Retro Audiometer Simulator

A browser-based clinical audiometer simulator that combines authentic 1970s equipment styling with modern AI-controlled autonomous testing capabilities.

### Core Purpose
- **Clinical Audiometry**: Implements standard Hughson-Westlake audiometric protocols for hearing threshold testing
- **AI Autonomy**: Fully autonomous test administration with real-time decision making and clinical explanations
- **Educational Tool**: Provides authentic clinical experience for training and research purposes
- **Retro Experience**: Authentic 1970s clinical equipment interface with period-appropriate styling and controls

### Key Features
- **Autonomous Testing**: AI clinician conducts complete bilateral audiometry without manual intervention
- **Clinical Accuracy**: Implements medical-grade protocols with proper ear isolation and calibrated audio
- **Malingering Detection**: Advanced algorithms detect false responses and non-organic hearing loss patterns
- **Real-time Audiogram**: Live plotting with confidence metrics and reliability assessment
- **Authentic Interface**: Rotary controls, LED indicators, CRT displays, and vintage hardware styling

### Target Users
- Audiology students and professionals
- Researchers studying hearing assessment
- Educational institutions teaching clinical audiometry
- Anyone interested in vintage medical equipment simulation

### Clinical Standards
- Follows IEC 60645-1 and ANSI S3.6 audiometer standards
- Implements Modified Hughson-Westlake procedure
- Provides clinical-grade channel isolation (>60 dB separation)
- Includes comprehensive false response detection and quality metrics
